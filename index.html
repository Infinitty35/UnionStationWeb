A community known for the way it cares for itself

Union Station South is a vibrant homeowners association in Broken Arrow, Oklahoma — recognized for its well-maintained properties, friendly neighbors, and a shared commitment to a high quality of life for every resident.

Whether you are new to the neighborhood or have been here for years, our property management team and HOA board work together to keep Union Station South a beautiful place to call home.
